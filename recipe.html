ANGELO'S RECIPE

CAKE SPECIAL RECIPE

This classic vanilla sponge cake is a true workhorse in the baking world. Its light and airy texture melts in your mouth, making it a perfect base for countless flavor variations and frosting combinations.Ah, the vanilla sponge cake. It may seem humble in its simplicity, but within its fluffy embrace lies a world of potential. This culinary blank canvas is a testament to the power of foundational baking techniques, offering a platform for endless creativity and flavor exploration.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs
