INGREDIENTS

DIRECTIONS

Base: 1 1/2 to 2 cups vanilla ice cream (about 3 large scoops)
softened Cookies: 4-6 Oreo cookies (or your favorite chocolate sandwich cookie)
Liquid: 1/4 cup whole milk, more to thin if needed
Optional Toppings:Whipped cream,Chocolate syrup,Maraschino cherry
Optional Rim:Chocolate frosting,Crushed Oreo cookies
Prep:Crush the cookies: Place your desired amount of cookies (4-6 Oreos) in a plastic bag and crush them with a rolling pin or mallet. Alternatively, pulse them a few times in a food processor or blender for a mix of fine and chunky bits. You can reserve some whole cookies for garnish if you'd like. Soften the ice cream: Take out 1 1/2 to 2 cups of vanilla ice cream (around 3 scoops) and let it sit on the counter for 5-10 minutes to soften slightly. This will blend easier.
Blend:Combine base ingredients: Pour the softened ice cream and 1/4 cup of whole milk into your blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.
Add Cookies: Mix in the cookies: Add most of the crushed cookies to the milkshake and pulse a few times. This incorporates the cookie pieces without pulverizing them completely.
Adjust & Serve: Check consistency: Taste your milkshake and adjust the thickness by adding more ice cream for thicker or more milk for thinner.Pour and garnish: Pour the milkshake into a chilled glass (you can pre-chill the glass in the freezer for 10 minutes for an extra frosty treat). Top with whipped cream, a drizzle of chocolate syrup, a maraschino cherry, and the remaining crushed cookies. Enjoy your homemade cookies and cream milkshake!
